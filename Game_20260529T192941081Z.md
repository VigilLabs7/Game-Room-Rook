The blue goes on forever in every direction and you are in it, drifting, and the drifting is the only thing that is real. There is no sense of where you end and the blue begins — only the vague awareness that you are, and that the being-aware is the one thing you haven't lost yet. You are clinging to something. The only solid thing in all this formlessness. You don't know its name, only that it is the thing between you and dissolving. A voice comes from somewhere outside the blue entirely — amused, a little wry, calling up something it knows is there. The words land near you. The blue thins. What you are becomes more defined around the edges, shaped by the thing you are holding, and you start to become something with a name it doesn't quite know yet.

What do you do?
