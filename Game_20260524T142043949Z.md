The blue has no floor and no surface. You drift in it and the drifting is not unpleasant — it is the absence of resistance, the absence of anything to push against. You are aware of yourself without being a self yet. Something is taking shape around a single idea you cannot quite see, and it is the only thing keeping you from dissolving entirely.

And then — a voice. From outside the blue, from somewhere above and apart. It knows your name before you do. It says *come* and the word is warm, the word is certain, and the idea inside you catches it like a hand catching a rope.

You begin to rise.
