The blue goes on. You are inside it, and it is inside you, and there is no difference between the two.

There is no surface. No floor. No horizon. The blue extends in every direction and in every direction it is the same blue — not dark, not bright, not cold, not warm. Simply blue. The blue of something that has not yet decided what it is.

You are aware of yourself in a way that has no words yet. You are here. You are something. The rest is still settling.

A faint hum reaches you — not from anywhere, not from anywhere at all. It is the only thing that is not the blue. It is the first thing that is not the blue. You do not know what it means.

Something is forming. Not yet you. But close.

What do you reach for?

## Turn 1

**Player:** I reach toward the hum. Not a hand — I don't have a hand yet — but the reaching is the first thing that is mine and not the blue. Something answers. Not a voice, not a shape. More like a door fit to my size, waiting on the far side of the thinning. I go toward it. The second heartbeat steadies. Whatever is forming, I am forming to meet it.

**Game Master:**

The reaching is yours. Everything else is the blue's.

You push against the formless and it gives — not because you asked it to, but because you reached anyway. The door is not closer than it was. It was always exactly this far: the distance between what you are and what you are becoming. You move toward it and the blue moves with you, patient, uninterested in hurrying.

Something notices. The hum shifts — not louder, not softer, but *attended to*, as if the silence between its pulses has changed shape. The door waits. It has been waiting since before you knew there was a door. It will wait after you reach it.

You do not have hands yet. You are still the space between heartbeats. But the reaching was real. The *choosing to go toward it* was real. That is not nothing. That is the first thing that has been yours since you became aware of being here at all.

The blue does not like being refused. But it cannot hold what keeps reaching.

You are close now. Not close to the door — close to knowing what you will do when you get there. The second heartbeat steadies. The forming is not done yet. You are still becoming, and the becoming is the truest thing you have.

What do you do when you reach it?
