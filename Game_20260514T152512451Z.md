Shapes move past, half-formed, carrying the shadows of lives not yet chosen. Something vast is gathering just beyond the edge of you. You are not anyone yet. Soon, that will change.

A harbor is taking shape in the grey, and at its edge a form is waiting, neither fully there nor fully absent. You carry your name like something heavy. You haven't spoken it yet. The fog thickens as you approach.
