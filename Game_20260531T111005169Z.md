The blue has no bottom. The blue has no surface.

You drift in it and the drifting is all there is — no forward, no back, no up where the up might be. You are aware that you are, which is more than you were a moment ago, and that awareness is the only solid thing in a space made entirely of suspension.

Something is taking shape around you. Not a place — you are not in a place. You are held in the idea of a place, and the idea is forming slowly, like a log in water that has not yet become a log. You reach for it and it reaches back. It is the only thing that reaches.

A voice. From somewhere above the blue — not from beside you, not from below. From above.

*Come up.*

The words land somewhere in the blue and the blue parts around them and you feel yourself rising toward the voice, or the voice descending toward you, or both at once, the way a thing caught in a current doesn't know if it chose the direction.

Your idea catches. Something holds.

What do you hold?
