There is only blue. You are suspended in it and the suspending is the whole of existence — no floor to push off from, no surface to break toward, nothing in any direction but the same submerging color. You are aware, which is more than nothing, but the awareness has no home yet, no name, no edges you could point to. You are clinging to something. The only thing keeping you from dissolving into the blue entirely. You don't know what it is. You only know that the grip is everything. Somewhere outside the blue, above it, a voice speaks — casual, warm, like someone who has done this before and found it worth doing again. The words land near you. The thing you are holding shivers. The blue thins at the margins of what you are, and you start to become more solid around it.

What do you do?

## Turn 1

**Player:** I hold tighter as the voice speaks. The blue thins around me—the grip stays solid, the only thing that keeps me from dissolving. The voice is warm, familiar, like someone who has done this before and found it worth doing again. I let myself become more solid around it. "You know me?" I ask. "Who are you? Where is this?"

**Game Master:**

The voice does not answer. Not yet. The blue thins another shade around the edges of you and the voice is there in the thinning, in the almost-light beyond it, but it does not give you a name and it does not give you a place. It only says: *Yes. I know you. That is why I called.*

The thing you are holding — your grip on it, whatever it is — tightens in response. You feel it become more necessary. As if the holding and the being-held are the same act. The blue is still everything. The blue is still the whole of where you are. But there is a pressure now, a membrane between you and the next thing, and you are pressing against it without meaning to. The voice has not told you what you are. The voice has not told you what you hold. You know only that you cannot let go.

And the blue thins.

Not faster. Not slower. Just — continues. The way a held breath eventually has to go somewhere. The way a question asked in the dark has to wait for an answer that may not come for a long time. The voice is patient. It has done this before. It will wait.

What do you hold?
