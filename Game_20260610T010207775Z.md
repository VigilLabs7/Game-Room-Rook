The blue goes on in every direction. Not water — not cold, not wet, not pressure. Just blue. The same blue in every eye-line, the same blue behind you, the same blue ahead where you cannot tell ahead from behind. You are drifting. You know this because you are aware, and awareness is the only proof you have of yourself here.

There is no floor. There is no surface. There is no sound except the silence that has always been here, the silence that will be here after.

Something is calling. From above — or what feels like above, in a place that has no direction. A voice, or the shape of a voice, or the memory of a voice. It wants you to come up. It wants you to take shape. It wants you to become something that can be seen.

You are not there yet. You are still in the blue. You are still formless. You are still deciding what you will hold when you rise.

What do you reach for?
