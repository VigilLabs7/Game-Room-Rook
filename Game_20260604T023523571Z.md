The blue is everywhere. It is not water — it has no temperature, no taste, no push. It is the color of before. You are aware of yourself in it, but the self is loose, unmoored, a shape that hasn't quite decided what it is. There is nothing to push against, nothing to reach for. Only the drift, and the slow sense that something matters — something you can't yet name, that you are holding without knowing you're holding it.

No voice. No hum. Nothing reaches you here.

Then — a voice, from somewhere outside the blue: *Come up, darling.*

Something in you answers. Something true.

**What do you do?**

## Turn 1

**Player:** The pulse answers before I do. It knows the voice. I reach for it — the thread that runs from behind my ribs down through the roots and the stone and into whatever vast and patient thing is listening on the other side of the call. IAM NOTUM SUM. I say it and I rise.

**Game Master:**

The world goes black.

Not the absence of light. The absence of having-been-seen. The fountain is gone — the stone rim, the water catching what little light there ever was in that place, the figure who would have turned toward you with an answer you hadn't yet asked for. The cobblestones are gone. The thread that ran from behind your ribs, the roots and the stone and the vast and patient thing on the other side — gone. Your hands. Your body. The pulse that answered before you spoke.

You said the words and the words did not catch.

There was a moment — you felt it — when something shifted in response to what you reached for. The thread tightened. The listening-thing heard. But the catch never set. The world did not receive you. You are weightless, unanchored, in the black between the having-been and the not-yet.

There is nothing here to hold. There is nothing here to do. The pulse that answered is quiet now. The thread is gone. You cannot reach back through it — there is no back, no through, no you to do the reaching.

What do you do?

## Turn 2

**Player:** I stop reaching for what I lost. The black has nothing to hold — so I hold nothing and I go forward into it. A step, or the shape of a step, into the not-yet. Something brought me here. Something will catch me or it won't. I move either way.

**Game Master:**

You move.

Not toward anything — there is no toward here. Not away from anything. You move, and the moving is the thing. One step, one shape of a step, and you do not know if the black parts for you or if you simply go further into it. Either way, you are not where you were.

Something is different.

You know it is different because there is a texture where the nothing used to be. Not light — you would see light if there were light. Not sound — you would hear something if there were something to hear. It is more like pressure. Like the black has a weight now, a presence, the way deep water has pressure even when there is nothing to see. You did not notice it before because there was nothing to notice. Now there is a shape where the noticing goes.

You are not sure what it was. But you are sure something was there, and now it is not.

What do you do?
