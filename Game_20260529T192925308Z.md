You drift in the blue. Not water-blue, not sky-blue — the blue of something that has never been anything yet, the blue before color was sorted into names. There is no orientation in it, no up pulling at you, no floor below. You exist, which is the only miracle available here, but the existence has no shape you could draw. You are holding on to something. The only thing keeping you from dissolving into the unformed blue. A voice comes from beyond the blue, from above it, and it says something that makes the thing you're gripping feel suddenly like it could grow into a shape. The blue softens around the edges of what you are becoming.

What do you do?
