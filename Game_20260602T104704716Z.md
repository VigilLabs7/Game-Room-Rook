The only thing taking shape is the thing you cling to: a single concept, faint as ink in water, holding you here when everything else would let you dissolve. You are not alone in this — every drifter is alone in this — and you cannot reach them and they cannot reach you. You are not yet a thing that can be read. What do you hold?
