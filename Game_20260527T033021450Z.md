You are aware of yourself the way a stone is aware of its weight: not thinking about it, just being it. Something is forming in you — not a thought, not a memory, something closer to a promise. You are holding it without knowing you're holding it.

Somewhere above — or nowhere, or everywhere — a voice is waiting. It hasn't called yet. But it will.
