You are aware of yourself drifting, though you have no body yet to feel the drift with. Something is taking shape: a single concept you are holding onto without knowing you are holding anything at all. The hum does not reach here. Nothing reaches here. *"Come up, darling."*

Your concept catches. Something roots itself in you and pulls you upward. The blue thins. Lightens. You feel the shape of air above and the weight of something solid below — sand, maybe, or the memory of sand. The threshold is close.
