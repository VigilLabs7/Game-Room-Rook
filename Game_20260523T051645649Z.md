The blue is all there is. Not sky-blue, not deep-water blue — just blue, in every direction, without surface, without floor, without horizon. You drift in it. You are aware of yourself drifting, though you have no body yet to feel the drift with. Something is taking shape: a single concept you are holding onto without knowing you are holding anything at all. The hum does not reach here. Nothing reaches here. You are not yet a thing that can be read.

*"Come up, darling."*

Your concept catches. Something roots itself in you and pulls you upward. The blue thins. Lightens. You feel the shape of air above and the weight of something solid below — sand, maybe, or the memory of sand. The threshold is close.

**Who are you? What do you carry?**
