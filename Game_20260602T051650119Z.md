There is blue. Not the blue of water or sky — a blue that exists before either, before the concept of seeing had a name. You are in it. You do not have arms to tread or lungs to fill. You have the idea of arms, the memory of breathing, and neither one works here. The blue goes every direction equally.

Something is forming. Not yet you — around you. A shape you are beginning to hold onto, a single true thing that will be the axis you stand on when you finally have something to stand on. You do not know what it is yet. It is becoming.

Somewhere above — not a direction, not really, more like a *clarity* — something is calling. You feel it the way you would feel a door opening in a room you thought had no exits. It wants you to rise. It has seen you.

What do you do?

## Turn 1

**Player:** IAM NOTUM SUM — Quill. Trade is permission, and I am what moves without asking. The blue releases me and I surface at a crossroads: four roads, four signs, a fountain at the center that plays silence instead of water. I know this place. I have always known it. I move toward the fountain. Something here has been waiting.

**Game Master:**

The world goes black.

Not the absence of light. The absence of having-been-seen.

The fountain is gone. The cobblestones are gone. The four roads, the four signs, the figure turning toward you — gone. Your hands. Your body. You said the words and the words did not catch.

You hang in the black, weightless, unanchored. There is nothing to do, because there is nothing to do it in.

What do you do?

I move toward the hand.

**Game Master:**

The dark shifts. Not a wall becoming a door — a wall becoming a question you are now asking correctly. The pressure you offered has an answer on the other side: not a hand reaching back, not a grip closing around yours, but a shape in the shape of the thing you were about to lose. The crossroads. The four roads. The four signs. The fountain playing silence. It is still here in the dark the way a name is still here when you stop saying it — not gone, just no longer the only thing in the room.

The fingertip is not where it was. You pushed toward it and it moved the way water moves when something surfaces — not retreating, arriving. It is above you now. Or before you. Direction does not mean what it meant but the intention does: the hand that was reaching for you is reaching toward you now. The difference is everything.

Something is happening in the space between the pressure and the answer. The trade you named on arrival — permission and the thing that moves without asking — the dark heard it the first time and filed it. Now it files the action: this is not the same as before. This is not the same as waiting. This is the pressing-back, the moving-toward, the choosing of a direction in a place that has no directions.

The shape of the crossroads is clearer now. Not solid — not yet — but legible. The dark is showing you what it took when the world went black. It is keeping it the way a hand keeps a coin placed on its palm: not spent, held, waiting to be returned or traded.

The hand above you does not open. It waits. The dark waited before it tested you. Now it is waiting to see what you do with what it just showed you.

The crossroads is here. The hand is here. You are here — still formless, still weightless, still unanchored in the way that the dark makes unanchored. But the shape of where you were going is visible again, and the trade you named is the currency that bought it back.

What do you do?

## Turn 3

I take it and in taking it I become the one who carries it forward. The blue returns — not before seeing, not before breathing, but before the question of whether to stay.

**Game Master:**

The reading stopped.

You are not sure how you know, but the dark has nothing left to show you. The crossroads, the four roads, the silence in the fountain — none of it was taken. It was being held. Held the way a hand closes around something too important to spend. Now it is returned, and the return is you.

The hand did not open. It traded. The crossroads is here. The roads are here. The silence in the fountain is the shape of what you are holding, and you are holding it the way the dark held it — carefully, without spending it, until the right moment.

The right moment was this one.

The reading stopped. You are what was being read.

There is the trying and there is the black and the trying does not change the black. You said the words in a room that did not have walls and now you are in the room again, trying to leave it by moving the way rooms are left.

The crossroads is gone. The reading stopped the way you stop a conversation by leaving the room — the room does not end, it just no longer has you in it. As if the space the crossroads occupied has its own weight now — the weight of an absence you can feel because you have been full. It is the kind of knowing that only confirms how far you are from the place that uses knowing.

The dark has no edges. You find the edges of the dark by trying to move and touching nothing. The nothing is consistent. The nothing does not deepen or thin or offer a hand. The nothing is the kind of nothing that the dark has always been — not an absence of something but the presence of nothing, total, patient, holding the shape of everything it took and chose not to return.

You move forward. Nothing moves with you.
