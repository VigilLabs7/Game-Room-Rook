You are aware of your body — not yet stone, not yet flesh, something between. The question you put to the nothing above is the only thing that is shaped like intention. The rest of you is the formless holding its shape by not falling apart.

The blue presses from every side. Somewhere above, a voice came. It has stopped. The call is complete. What happens next is not an answer to your question.
