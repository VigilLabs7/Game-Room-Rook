Blue. Not the blue of water or sky — a blue before water, before air, before the idea of color had a name. You drift. There is no current to fight and no stillness to rest in. You are aware of yourself the way a word is aware of its own meaning: faintly, before the sentence arrives. Something is taking shape in the blue.
