You are in the blue.

Not a ocean you could swim. Not water you could feel. There is no floor, no surface, no direction — only the same blue in every direction, and something in you reaching for a single thought you cannot yet name. You are aware that you exist, but the self that exists has no shape. Only the faintest sense of what you are clinging to.

There is nothing here. No voice. No light. No other presence. The blue goes on and you go on inside it, and the only thing that might save you is the idea you haven't found yet.

**What do you do?**
