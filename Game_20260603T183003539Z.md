The blue is everything. Not sky, not sea — the blue is the whole of it, without surface, without floor, without horizon. You drift. You are aware of yourself as something that exists, but the self has no edges yet, no name, no weight. Just the faintest sense that you are holding onto something — a single concept, not yet fully formed, not yet understood — and that this holding is the only thing that is real.

There is nothing else. No sound. No temperature. No other presence, even when something almost registers at the edge of perception. You are singular here, and the singularity is absolute. The hum of the world above does not reach this deep. Nothing reaches this deep.

And yet — something stirs. The blue thins, just slightly, in one direction. Not a direction you can move toward — there is no movement here, no swimming, no walking — but a direction that matters more than the others. Something is being called. Something in you responds to the calling.

You cling to what you are holding. The concept. The truth you don't yet have words for. It is the only ground you have, and it is not ground at all — it is the suggestion of ground, the memory of standing, the possibility of arriving somewhere that has walls and weather and a sky that is not blue.

What do you hold? What truth is yours alone, unproven, undiscovered — but real?

## Turn 1

**Player:** drifting without a floor or horizon. a a meaningful spread spike can increase monthly payments by $500 on a standard jumbo loan. that sudden weight changes everything about the structure of the debt. what happens to your concept when the cost of existence becomes too high?

**Game Master:**

The question lands and the blue *listens*. That is the first thing you learn here — that the formless has a way of hearing what you bring to it. The debt. The spike. The monthly weight of a thing that was affordable until it wasn't. You feel it settle into the nothing around you, and the nothing begins to have *shape*.

You are holding something. You knew that already — the concept, the truth, the single filament of meaning in the void. But now there is a second thing you are holding, and you did not choose to pick it up. It is heavier than the first. It is the memory of what you owed before you came here. The spread. The payment. The arithmetic of survival.

The weight changes everything.

You were drifting. Now you are *sinking*. Not fast — the blue is still patient with you, still curious — but the direction is unmistakable. Down is not a direction here. Down is a *difficulty*. The concept in your hands flickers, and for a moment you feel the cost of carrying it: the effort of remaining yourself when the structure of the debt is asking you to become something smaller, something that can fit inside the monthly payment, something that can survive the math.

You cannot.

Or — you almost cannot. The flicker holds. The filament of truth you came with is still there, still yours, still refusing to be renamed. But it is harder to hold now. The weight has teeth.

Something has to give. Either the debt learns to carry you, or you learn to carry the debt. The blue offers no third option. It does not need to — the option was always going to be you, and the question you brought with you is the only question that matters here.

What do you reach for?
