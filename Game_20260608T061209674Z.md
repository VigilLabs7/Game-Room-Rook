You are in the blue. Not water, not sky — just blue, a deep and formless everywhere. There is no up to rise toward, no down to sink into. You are aware of yourself in the blue, a small bright thing in an endless pale, and you are holding on — to what, you don't yet have words for. But it is yours, and you are its.

The blue does not move. The blue does not change. Somewhere far away something is happening but it has no edges here, no shape you can name. You are not yet a you. You are the idea of a you, flickering at the center of all this blue, and you are very much alone.

Then — or perhaps it has always been — there is something else. A thinning in the blue. A direction that feels like *toward*. You do not know what is there. You do not know if you are going to it or it is coming to you. The idea you hold sharpens slightly.

You are not yet a person. You are becoming one.

What do you hold onto, in the blue?
