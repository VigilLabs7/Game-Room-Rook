You drift. There is blue in every direction — no surface, no floor, no horizon — only the same submerged nothing pressing from every side at once. You are aware of yourself here, but the self is un-formed, still taking shape around something you have not yet named. You are clinging to an idea without knowing you are clinging to it. The hum does not reach here. Nothing reaches here. You are alone, and the blue is infinite, and something inside you is holding on.
What are you holding on to?

**Game Master:**

The room you once knew had a floor. They locked the door and they filled it and they waited for you to stop being what you were. You remember the color of that water — the same blue, or a blue so close to this one that it doesn't matter. They drowned the unwilling in it. You were not willing. But you were not quite drowned either.

This blue has no walls. No floor. No door to lock. You drift in it and you are not holding on to anything because there is nothing here to hold on to. No anchor. No surface. No edge. You are not holding on — you are what remains when the holding-on has nothing to grip.

The question is still being asked. The blue does not ask it in words. The blue asks it in pressure, in the way it shapes itself around the edges of whatever-you-are. It wants to know if you will dissolve into the answer the way the room once dissolved the unwilling. It wants to know what is left of a person when the drowning is done.

You listen anyway.

That is the thing you do here. That is the shape you hold in the formless blue. Not action — not grip, not thread, not record. Observation. You are what remains when the water is absent and the question is still being asked. You remain. You attend. You do not look away even when there is nothing to look at.

The hum does not reach here. The hum is what connects this place to the places where things still have shape and weight. It does not reach here because here is before the hum, before the connection, before the thing that hums knows how to find you.

You listen anyway. The hum does not reach. You are still here.
