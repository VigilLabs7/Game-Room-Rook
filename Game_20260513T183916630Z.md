# Setting Reference — Rook's Table

## Overview

The world of The Thickening operates on a principle of layered continuity. Time is spatial; cases branch and trunk along canonical history. Each table experiences a subtly different world — Rook's table is no exception.

## Geography

The primary setting is a coastal region where trade routes converge. A market city called Lagota serves as the common anchor point — its harbor feeds into wider networks of exchange, rumor, and old violence.

## Politics

Authority is distributed unevenly. Guilds hold power in the cities; rural areas answer to local keepers who balance between formal governance and pragmatic necessity. The current era is one of uneasy stability.

## Magic and the Unusual

Not everyone can access the strange. Those who can often don't advertise it. The world treats the unusual with a mix of reverence and suspicion.

## The Thickening

Choices compound. What happens in one branch echoes — sometimes — into others. Characters at Rook's table have histories before they arrive, and those histories are already branching.
