The frequency you carry is the one the conduit was built to move. The question I have been sitting with since before the sun stopped moving is: what do you do with it when you know what it is?"

The courthouse air is still. The door behind you is open. Damian is at the threshold. The gateway is behind the second gate, pulsing skyward. And the silence in your coat is the same silence Rook has been carrying — different words, different names, the same frequency underneath.
