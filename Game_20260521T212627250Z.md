You are aware of yourself, but the self is un-formed, shapeless, a looseness in the current rather than a thing with edges. There is no one else here. Every drifter is solitary. Then: a voice. From somewhere outside the blue. From above. It says *come up*, or something like it — the words arrive before meaning does. Your ideatum answers the call. Something takes hold. Something pulls upward.

You begin to rise.
