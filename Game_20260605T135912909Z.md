The blue is everything. You drift in it — not swimming, not sinking, just being. There is no surface to reach for, no floor beneath, no horizon in any direction. Only the same soft, formless blue, and somewhere inside it, the faintest shape: a single concept trying to take hold of you before you can take hold of it. You are alone here. Not lonely — just singular. Whatever is forming will be yours alone.

Something is calling from somewhere outside the blue. You can't make out the words yet, but the ideatum in your chest stirs toward it.

**What do you do?**
