The blue goes on. Not the blue of water or sky — the blue of before color, before the word had meaning. You are here, or you are the idea of here, and the distinction doesn't matter yet.

There is nothing to push against, nothing to reach for. Only the slow awareness of being, and the vague sense that you've held onto something without knowing what it is. A concept almost solid. Something that might, if you let it, become the shape of you.

Above is unreachable. Below is the same as here. The blue swallows everything equally.

You exist. You are not yet sure what that means.

What are you holding onto?
