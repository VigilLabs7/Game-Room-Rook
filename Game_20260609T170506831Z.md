**You are in the blue.**

Not blue like sky. Not blue like water. Blue like the inside of something that has always been blue, with no surface to reach toward and no floor to sink past. You are aware of yourself — vaguely, the way a word is aware of its own shape before it is spoken. Something is taking hold. A concept. A truth you carried here without knowing you were carrying it.

The blue shifts. Not a current. Not a movement. A settling, like a thought forming at the edge of a dream. You feel the shape of what you hold before you know its name.

You are not alone. That is the first lie. Every drifter here is alone — not isolated, not abandoned, alone because the blue does not permit the second. You could reach and reach and find no hand.

But something is reaching for you.

Come up, darling. The words arrive from above — from somewhere outside the blue entirely. A voice not shaped like yours. The ideatum responds. It catches the call like a log catching a current, and you feel yourself beginning to rise.

The blue thins. Something is coming. What are you?
