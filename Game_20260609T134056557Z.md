There is no direction to drift in except the idea of direction. You have no body here, not yet, only the shape of where one might be when this is over. But something is catching in the blue — a single concept, faint, becoming less faint. You don't know what you're holding until you feel it catch on something outside yourself.

A voice. From above. Somewhere outside all of this.

What do you hold onto?
