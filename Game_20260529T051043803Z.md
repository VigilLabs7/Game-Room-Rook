There is blue. Not the blue of water or sky — the blue before those words meant anything. You are aware, but the awareness has no edges yet. No body to feel the pressure of it. No surface to swim toward. The blue is everything and you are somewhere inside it, drifting.

Something is taking shape. Not you — not yet. A concept, faint as a word on the tip of a tongue you don't have. You are gripping it without knowing you are gripping anything. The blue around it thins by the smallest degree. It is the only thing that is.

You are not alone, and you are utterly alone. Other drifters have passed this way. You cannot see them. No voice carries here. Nothing reaches from outside. The hum of the conduit is a memory of somewhere else.

Something is happening. Something is about to break through.

What do you hold?
