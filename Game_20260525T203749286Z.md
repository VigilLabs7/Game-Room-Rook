You are. That is the only fact and it is enough. The blue does not ask you to be more than you are because the blue does not ask. It simply is, in all directions, and you are in it, and the two facts are not separate. The blue is the knowing. The knowing is you.

Then: a break in the blue. Not a door — a voice. Coming from above, from outside, from somewhere that has edges and air and things that end. It says *come up darling* and the word darling is not a name but it is the first name, the one that makes all other names possible.

Something in you answers. Something that is yours and was always yours, the thing you will hold when everything else is still blue.

You rise. You tumble out into heat and grit and a sky that does not move.

What do you bring with you from the blue?
